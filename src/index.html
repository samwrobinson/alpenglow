About Me

Finding a Path Forward

Rooted in the Green Mountains of Vermont, my therapy practice provides a space for individuals to have their lived experiences mirrored back to them with new tools and insight. I offer in-person counseling in downtown Burlington, Vermont, virtual appointments, and seasonal outdoor sessions.

  • Specializing life transitions, anxiety, grief, and trauma.

Why I Became a Therapist

Over the past decade, I've worked in a variety of roles where my task was to create a safe space. From outdoor education to adventure therapy and residential settings, I noticed how difficult it was for people to find momentum or settle into goal-focused work when they did not feel regulated in their environment. We live in a fast-paced world and I aim to create and calm environment, whether we meet virtually, in-person, or in nature, so you can slow down and work on what matters to you most.
