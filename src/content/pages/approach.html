My Approach

Together we can

Step Into Change

I believe that every individual’s journey is unique, and so is the path to healing. Whether you’re struggling with anxiety, trauma, or a difficult life transition, my goal is to empower you to find clarity and resilience. Together, we’ll work at a pace that feels right for you, in a non-judgmental environment that fosters self-discovery and personal growth.

Change can bring up feeling of uncertainty and even fear. I work with clients to understand the origins of their relationship to change and provide strategies for preparing to make meaningful change. Whether you're looking to change the way you communicate with others in your life or shift your connection to self, our work together can help you course correct and stay on track.

Together we will explore change by:

  1. Understanding your readiness for change.
  2. Discovering what motivates you to try something different.
  3. Identifying challenges related to executive functioning including task initiation, planning, organization, and execution.
  4. Unpacking the origins of your core beliefs and how they relate to change.

Learn to trust your internal resources and wisdom

I believe that the individuals I work with have the innate ability to know what is best for them. Sometimes life presents us with challenges that cause us to stray from our truth. I work with clients to help them understand what pulls them away from meeting their own needs.

Together, we will explore patterns and systems including:

  • Perfectionism
  • Pleasing Others
  • Guilt and shame
  • Family of origin and younger parts of the self
A Word From Amber

Together, we will identify your current challenges and utilize evidence-based therapeutic modalities to help you progress towards your goals. I aim to provide my clients with strategies they can make their own and feel confident in utilizing outside of sessions.
