Offerings and Resources

Alpenglow Counseling

Offerings

In the winter months, I offer cross country ski sessions integrating EMDR. Bilateral movement is a key processing component of EMDR and cross country skiing is an ideal rhythmic and repetitive activity that can aid the reprocessing journey.

Whether you are interested or experienced in cross country skiing, we can co-create an experience conducive to your therapeutic process. Please indicate your interest in this modality when reaching out to connect with me.
